The Battle of Biblio Hill, August 5, 1943

Bibilo Hill was strategically significant because it provided a commanding view of the Munda airstrip. After heavy fighting, U.S. forces captured the hill, which allowed them to establish control over the airfield and use it as a base for further operations against Japanese positions in the Northern Solomons.
