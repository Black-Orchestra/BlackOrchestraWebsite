The Battle of Horson

Located in the gulf of Finland, Horson is the UBoat Vesikko's base of operations. The base is built by the Finnish Navy and utilised by Germany during the Continuation War. The base is currently under Kriegmarines operation, backed by a powerful Heer echelon.

The map features a Soviet naval landing force making an attempt to secure the island. Lend-lease equipments are heavily used in an enviroment they were not designed for.
